IF Temperatura is frio AND  Humedad  is baja and Viento is calmado  then Sensacion is frio  
IF Temperatura is frio and Humedad is alta AND Viento is calmado then Sensacion is normal
if Temperatura is normal and Humedad is regular and Viento is calmado then Sensacion is normal
if Temperatura is normal and Humedad is alta  and NOt Viento is intenso then Sensacion is caliente
IF Temperatura is normal and (Viento is intenso or Viento is moderado) then Sensacion is frio
IF Temperatura is caliente and Viento is intenso then Sensacion is normal
if Temperatura is caliente AND (Viento is moderado or Viento is calmado) then Sensacion is caliente
